Dal Rice

Recipe:

  1. Put Olive Oil on the Pan
  2. Add Cumin Seeds
  3. Add 2 - 3 Red Chillies or Flakes
  4. Add curry leaves
  5. Add 2-3 Garlic pieces
  6. Add some diced onions
  7. Add 2 - 3 Green Chillies or Flakes
  8. Add Salt
  9. Add Turmeric powder or the yellowness
  10. Add Dal (Mysore Dal)
  11. Pour some water to it and let it cook
  12. Enjoy!
